○   The studio

Tailored interiors,
delivered on time.

Smart Renovation is a Dubai design-build studio for villas, apartments and offices. We bring European editorial sensibility together with Dubai execution precision — one team, one fixed price, one fixed date, from first sketch to final handover.

Mission

To make the renovation experience clear, calm, and predictable — and the result quietly extraordinary.

Promise

Fixed price in 7 days. On-time handover guaranteed. No surprises.

Audience

Discerning private clients and family offices renovating villas, penthouses and offices across the Emirates.

Pillars

Discipline · Editorial restraint · Material honesty · End-to-end accountability.

02 · Logo04 / 12
○   Wordmark

The mark.

The Smart Renovation wordmark is set in Inter Medium, all lowercase, with a -1.5% optical letter-spacing. It is a wordmark — not a logo with an icon. The lowercase form expresses calm authority; the absence of decoration expresses discipline.

Primary use

Ink on bone (#111110 on #FFFFFF). Default for digital and print.

Paper variant

Ink on paper (#F4F2ED). For warm contexts, stationery, packaging.

Reversed

Bone on ink. For dark backgrounds and photography overlays only.

○   Clearspace & minimum size

Give it room
to breathe.

↑ x ↑ x ↓ x ↓ x smart renovation

Minimum clearspace on all sides equals the cap-height ("x") of the wordmark. Never place text, edges, or other marks closer than this distance.

Minimum size · digital
smart renovation

120 px wide / 13 px cap-height. Below this, legibility breaks down.

Minimum size · print
smart renovation

25 mm wide. Pair with a generous margin; never let it touch a fold or edge.

Two inks.
Two papers.

Pairing rule

Always pair Ink with Bone or Paper. Never combine Ink with Night, or Paper with Bone in the same composition. Reversed pairings (Bone on Night) are reserved for photography overlays and the dark digital theme.

07 · Imagery09 / 12
○   Art direction

Quiet rooms.
Honest light.

Photography is the brand's loudest voice. Every image is editorial — composed, daylit, and unstaged. We shoot rooms as they're meant to be lived in. No props, no lifestyle clichés, no aerial drone tropes.

hero — palm jumeirah villa, living room
detail — kitchen, calacatta
detail — entry hall
Do
  • · Natural daylight, single source where possible
  • · 35mm or 50mm equivalent — no wide-angle distortion
  • · Compose to the architecture: lines straight, ceilings level
  • · Show finished, lived-in rooms (not staged showrooms)
  • · Crop tight on materials, joints, light play
Don't
  • · No HDR or heavily edited skies
  • · No people in frame for hero shots
  • · No aerial drone establishing shots
  • · No stock photography, ever
  • · No filters, vignettes, or warm-tone overlays
